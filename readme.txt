== Description ==
Rex Ad Cards is the easiest way to serve, manage and track your own ads. Use it to promote your own products or the products of your sponsors. You will need to create an account with https://www.rexadcards.com/ to use Rex Ad Cards on Wordpress.

== Installation ==
FROM WITHIN WORDPRESS

Visit ‘Plugins > Add New’
Search for ‘Rex Ad Cards’
Activate Rex Ad Cards from your Plugins page.
Go to “after activation” below.

MANUALLY

Upload the rex-ad-cards folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
Activate the Rex Ad Cards plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress
Go to “after activation” below.

AFTER ACTIVATION

From the Settings menu, click on Rex Ad Cards
Follow the instructions to add your Rex Ad Cards snippet to Wordpress.
Copy the shortcode and paste it anywhere you want the Rex Ad Card to show
You’re done!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
Can I signup for a Rex Ad Cards account from the Wordpress plugin?
No, at this time you can only create an account on https://www.rexadcards.com directly

Can I update my card from the Wordpress admin panel?
Not at this time, for now please go to https://www.rexadcards.com to update your card
